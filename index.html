Welcome to JPHES Land

What is JPHES

It is a portal built on DHIS2 that streamlines health data reporting where all partners can access up to date attributed data from the same database instance unlike the previous scenario where each partner was running their own KePMS instance.In addition, it facilitates automatic data pull and attribution from the national DHIS2 to avoid double data entry.

More Information

JPHES is based on two systems, the Joint Partners Reporting Platform(JPRP) and Public Health & Environment System(PHES).

JPRP is a DHIS2 App that facilitates automatic data pull from the national DHIS2, data attribution by partners and programs, and data push to DATIM

PHES facilitates data approval,analysis and visualization of data.
